# Privacy Policy for WaterMinder

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

WaterMinder ("we", "our", or "us") provides a browser extension designed to help users track their hydration and set reminders. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle functionality and data within the "WaterMinder" extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
WaterMinder **does not collect, transmit, or share** any personal information, usage data, or analytics. We do not have servers, and we do not track your browsing history or behavior.

## 2. Data Storage
All data generated by your use of the extension is stored **locally** on your device using the browser's storage capabilities (`chrome.storage.local`). This includes:
- Your daily water intake logs.
- Your daily goal settings.
- Your reminder preferences (intervals and notification settings).
- Your streak count.

This data is strictly confined to your browser instance and allows the extension to function offline.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties.

## 4. Permissions
WaterMinder requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:
- **`alarms`**: To schedule recurrent notifications for water reminders.
- **`notifications`**: To display visual alerts when it is time to drink water.
- **`storage`**: To save your preferences and logs locally on your device.
